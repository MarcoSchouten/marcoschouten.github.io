Marco Schouten

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the Technical University of Denmark affiliated with the Pioneer Centre For AI and part of the Belongie Lab. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Dimitrios Papadopoulos and Director and Prof. Serge Belongie.

Previously, I was a research intern at SMaRC working on underwater vehicle pose estimation, and at Aptiv working on HD map generation from point clouds.

Research Interests

My research focuses on deep learning in human-machine collaboration. Currently, I am working on generative models for images and text, and novel ways to control them.
