Marco Schouten

I am a PhD student at the Technical University of Denmark affiliated with the Pioneer Centre For AI and part of the Belongie Lab. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Dimitrios Papadopoulos and Director and Prof. Serge Belongie.

My research focuses on deep learning in human-machine collaboration. Currently, I am working on generative models for images and text.

Previously, I worked on sonar-based pose estimation at the Swedish Maritime Robotic Centre and gained industry experience in HD map generation at APTIV.
