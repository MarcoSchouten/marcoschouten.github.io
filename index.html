Marco Schouten

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the Technical University of Denmark, affiliated with the Pioneer Centre For AI and part of the Belongie Lab. I am deeply grateful for the guidance of my advisors, Prof. Dimitrios Papadopoulos and Director and Prof. Serge Belongie, whose mentorship inspires my work.

Previously, I was a research intern in computer vision at SMaRC and Aptiv.

Research Interests

My research focuses on deep learning in human-machine collaboration. I am particularly interested in generative models for images and text, exploring creative applications in the arts and novel ways to control these systems.
