Marco Schouten

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the Technical University of Denmark, affiliated with the Pioneer Centre For AI and part of the Belongie Lab. I am fortunate to be advised Prof. Dim Papadopoulos and Prof. Serge Belongie. Previously, I was a research intern at the Swedish Maritime Robotic Centre and Aptiv PLC.

Research Interests

My research focuses on human-in-the-loop deep learning, with an emphasis on generative models and stable diffusion. I study methods for personalization and control to adapt these models to user needs.
