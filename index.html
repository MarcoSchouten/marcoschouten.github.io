Marco Schouten

I am a PhD student at the Technical University of Denmark affiliated with the Pioneer Centre For AI and part of the Belongie Lab. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Dimitrios Papadopoulos and Director and Prof. Serge Belongie.

My research focuses on deep learning within the context of human-machine collaboration, in particular I am interested generative models for images and text.

Previosuly I worked on optimization Swedish Maritime Robotic Centre under the guidance of Prof. John Folkesson. I also had an industry experience at APTIV, focusing on deep learning and computer vision for HD map generation.
